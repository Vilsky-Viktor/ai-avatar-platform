How it works

Two ways to create your avatar

Whether you want a perfect digital replica of yourself or a completely new synthetic persona — both paths lead to the same result: a hyper-realistic AI avatar ready to create content.

Digital twin

Clone yourself

Upload 7 carefully composed ID photos following our instructions. The platform processes them and builds a hyper-realistic digital replica of you.

1
Upload 7 ID photos following the provided guide
2
Platform processes and validates your photos
3
Your digital twin is ready to generate content
Synthetic

Design your persona

Choose from a diverse set of appearance settings to design a unique synthetic avatar that doesn't exist in the real world.

1
Pick appearance settings from a curated list
2

3

Features

Everything you can create

Six powerful ways to produce content with your avatar — from a single prompt to a full photo and video series.

Image

Generate photos

Describe any scene and your avatar appears in it. Add up to 3 reference images and refer to them directly in the prompt for precise composition control.

Video · Audio

Generate videos

Create videos with your avatar in any scene. Optionally add an object with up to 4 reference photos, and add voice via text-to-speech or browser mic recording.

🎞️
Motion

Mimic motion

Upload any reference video and your avatar mirrors every movement and gesture. Optionally keep the original audio from the source video.

Image · Sets

Photosets

Pick a predefined theme and generate a complete photo series in one click — WhatsApp stickers, outfit styles, around the world, luxury life, and more.

🎙️
Audio

Generate audio

Type any text and your avatar's assigned voice speaks it. No video — just high-quality AI voice generation using the latest state-of-the-art models.

Our philosophy

Simplicity is the key

01
Avatar creation takes just 5 minutes

Upload 7 photos or pick your appearance settings — your avatar is ready in minutes. No waiting hours, no manual configuration.

02
Simple and intuitive navigation

Everything is where you expect it to be. Pick what you want to create, describe it, and generate — no learning curve required.

03
Minimum settings. Maximum intelligence.

We run over 40 AI models behind the scenes to understand exactly what you want and produce the best possible result — without asking you to configure anything.

04
You don't pick models. We do.

No dropdowns full of model names. No prompt engineering guides. Just describe what you want — we handle all the technical decisions invisibly.

05
Minimalistic design

A clean, distraction-free interface that keeps the focus on your content — not on the tools used to create it.

06
MVP is fully developed and ready

The platform is built. Every feature works end to end. We are 3 months away from going live — final testing, infrastructure scaling, and polish.

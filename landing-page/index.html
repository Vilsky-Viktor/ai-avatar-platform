Features

Infinite creativity

Five powerful ways to produce content with your avatar — from a single prompt to a full photo, video and audio series.

Image

Generate photos

Describe any scene and your avatar appears in it. Add up to 3 reference images and refer to them directly in the prompt for precise composition control.

Video · Audio

Generate videos

Create videos with your avatar in any scene. Optionally add an object with up to 4 reference photos, and add voice via text-to-speech or browser mic recording.

Motion

Mimic motion

Upload any reference video and your avatar mirrors every movement and gesture. Optionally keep the original audio from the source video.

Image · Sets

Photosets

Pick a predefined theme and generate a complete photo series in one click — WhatsApp stickers, outfit styles, around the world, luxury life, and more.

Audio

Generate audio

Type any text and your avatar's assigned voice speaks it. With video lipsync or just high-quality AI voice generation using 12,000 available voices in multiple languages.
